主题
Accent

主题通过 .dark 切换，Accent 通过 .accent-* 或 data-accent-color 切换；组件可在自身容器覆写。

尺寸变体

Size 1 (Compact)

This is a compact callout message. Perfect for inline notifications or small hints.

Size 2 (Default)

This is a default callout message. Suitable for general information and warnings.

Size 3 (Large)

This is a large callout message. Best for important announcements or detailed information.

代码示例

<!-- 基本用法 -->
<div class="callout-root callout-size-2 callout-variant-soft">
  <div class="callout-icon">
    <svg>...</svg>
  </div>
  <div class="callout-text">
    Your message here
  </div>
</div>

<!-- 不同尺寸 -->
<div class="callout-root callout-size-1 callout-variant-soft">...</div>
<div class="callout-root callout-size-2 callout-variant-soft">...</div>
<div class="callout-root callout-size-3 callout-variant-soft">...</div>

<!-- 不同变体 -->
<div class="callout-root callout-size-2 callout-variant-soft">...</div>
<div class="callout-root callout-size-2 callout-variant-surface">...</div>
<div class="callout-root callout-size-2 callout-variant-outline">...</div>

<!-- 高对比度 -->
<div class="callout-root callout-size-2 callout-variant-soft callout-high-contrast">
  ...
</div>

<!-- 自定义颜色 -->
<div data-accent-color="red">
  <div class="callout-root callout-size-2 callout-variant-soft">
    <div class="callout-icon"><svg>...</svg></div>
    <div class="callout-text">Error message</div>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- 无图标 -->
<div class="callout-root callout-size-2 callout-variant-soft">
  <div class="callout-text">Message without icon</div>
</div>
