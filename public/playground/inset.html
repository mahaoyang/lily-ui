Inset

Applies negative margins to allow content to bleed into surrounding padding.

Basic Usage

Inset allows content like images to extend to the edges of a container despite its padding.

Without Inset

Card content with normal padding.

With Inset (side: all)

Image extends to card edges.

Side Options

Control which sides the inset applies to.

side: top

Header extends to top & sides

Content below with normal padding.

side: bottom

Content above with normal padding.

Footer extends to bottom & sides

side: left

Left

Content with sidebar extending to left edge.

side: x (horizontal)

Normal content above.

Extends left & right only

Normal content below.
