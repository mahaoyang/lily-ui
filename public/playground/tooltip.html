Features

  • Hover activation - Shows on mouse hover with configurable delay
  • Keyboard accessible - Also shows on focus for keyboard navigation
  • Multiple positions - Supports top, right, bottom, and left positioning
  • Customizable delay - Configure show and hide delays to match your UX
  • Smooth animations - Elegant slide and fade animations
